Overview

NUB Business Entrepreneurship And Leadership Club (BEL Club) is a dynamic platform at Northern University Bangladesh (NUB) aimed at fostering leadership and entrepreneurial skills among students. As a business-oriented club, it provides opportunities for students to engage in practical learning experiences through workshops, seminars, competitions, and networking events. The club's mission is to inspire and nurture the next generation of business leaders and entrepreneurs by promoting innovation, teamwork, and strategic thinking. BEL Club serves as a bridge between academic knowledge and real-world business challenges, empowering members to enhance their business acumen and leadership capabilities.
